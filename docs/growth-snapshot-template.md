# Weekly Growth Snapshot Template

## How to Use

Create one copy per week under `analysis/` using this filename pattern:

```text
analysis/growth-snapshot-YYYY-MM-DD.md
```

The snapshot should take 15-20 minutes. The goal is not perfect analytics. The goal is to force a weekly decision: continue, adjust, or investigate.

## Decision

Choose one:

- Continue: current direction has clear positive signal.
- Adjust: traffic exists but landing page, content angle, or tool UX needs change.
- Investigate: signal is unclear; collect one more week or inspect a specific issue.
- Pause: no meaningful signal after repeated distribution and indexing checks.
